About Us

Our mission statement

"Our goal is to provide students with the opportunity to explore scientific concepts and use creativity to solve problems."

What we are looking for:

creativity !

Requirements:

Students/teams must register on the Google Form found on the "Sign Up" page
Every student/team needs to create a lab report and display to be exhibited in the science fair.
Lab reports should consist of an introduction, methods section, results, and conclusion.

Projects will judged based off FIVE main sections: introduction with a hypothesis, materials, methods/ experimentation, data/ results, and conclusion.
Sources should be cited in APA format in a bibliography

Projects will be judges based on creativity, understanding of scientific principles, and the potential application of the research.
Projects will be judged with respect to each grade
There will be one winner in each grade with two runner-ups.
Prizes vary based on grade level.
